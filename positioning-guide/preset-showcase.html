🧮 Positioning Formulas

Understanding how device positioning is calculated.

// Caption Position: above
deviceTop = captionHeight + (canvasHeight - captionHeight - deviceHeight) × framePosition/100

// Caption Position: below  
deviceTop = (canvasHeight - captionHeight - deviceHeight) × framePosition/100

// Caption Position: overlay
deviceTop = (canvasHeight - deviceHeight) × framePosition/100

Pro Tips:
• Use framePosition: 25 for Text Heavy presets to keep device close to caption
• Watch devices work best with frameScale: 0.6-0.9 and smaller captions
• Overlay captions save vertical space but require good background contrast
• Border + background creates the strongest caption emphasis
• Test your screenshots at actual App Store display sizes
